Key Components

Anticipation and Preparedness

Communities must anticipate future climate risks such as storms, droughts, or heatwaves, and plan accordingly.

Adaptation Strategies

Adaptation involves making changes to systems, infrastructure, and practices to reduce vulnerability to climate impacts.

Recovery and Rebuilding

Resilience also includes the ability to recover quickly after a disaster, ensuring that communities can rebuild stronger.

Best Tools

Climate Resilient Cities Tool
(CRCTool)

A free software tool that helps with urban planning and design for climate adaptation. It includes a database of over 50 adaptation measures, including nature-based solutions. Users can sketch out where to apply the measures, calculate their effects, and compare options.

Climate Resilience Evaluation
and Awareness Tool

A tool from the US EPA that helps with climate resilience planning. It includes features for:
Climate awareness
Scenario development
Consequences and assets
Adaptation planning
Risk assessment

U.S. Climate Resilience
Toolkit

A framework that helps communities consider and address climate hazards. It includes a portal that allows communities to document their exposure to climate hazard
